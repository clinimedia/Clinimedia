Dental marketing in Burlington

Our headquarters. Roughly 80 active dental clinics within a 10 km radius of CliniMedia's Fraser Drive office. Our home-court market — and where the deepest pool of CliniMedia client clinics sits.

Dental marketing in Oakville

Fifteen minutes east of our office. High-income corridor along Lakeshore and the Trafalgar/Dundas commercial belt — strong demand for cosmetic, Invisalign, and pediatric specialty marketing.

Dental marketing in Mississauga

Roughly 30 minutes east into Peel Region. The most linguistically diverse of our markets — multi-language ad creative and culturally specific content production are routinely part of a Mississauga clinic engagement.

Dental marketing in Hamilton

Twenty-five minutes south-west into Hamilton-Wentworth. The cost-per-lead in Hamilton typically runs 20 to 30 percent below GTA-core averages, which makes Hamilton clinics some of the highest-ROI partners we work with.

Dental marketing in Milton

One of Canada's fastest-growing municipalities. Milton clinics often lean heavily on Google Ads (search-led) since the local population is still in the "establishing a regular dentist" phase rather than the "considering switching" phase.

Dental marketing in Toronto

The toughest auction prices in Ontario. Toronto clinics succeed with a heavier weight on organic — local SEO, Google Business Profile, and Reels content — because paid search cost-per-clicks are 2 to 3x the rest of the GTA.
