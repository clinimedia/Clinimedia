Dental and Medical Marketing Services in the Greater Toronto Area.

CliniMedia provides six core services for dental and medical clinics: content and media day production, Meta and Google Ads management, clinic website design and SEO, Google reviews and Google Business Profile management, email and SMS marketing automation, and a centralized communication platform with optional chatbot.

Service packages are offered at three tiers. Silver is a starter ad-management plan. Gold adds social media management, monthly Google Business Profile updates, and two on-site media days a year. Diamond is full-service growth including reputation management, email and SMS automations, monthly email campaigns, four media days a year, website design and SEO, and chatbot setup with Google Ads integration.

All services are delivered by an in-house team based in Burlington, Ontario, serving dental and medical clinics across Burlington, Oakville, Mississauga, Hamilton, Milton, Toronto, Etobicoke, Brampton, and Vaughan.
